Victoria Memorial

Situated in Kolkata, this is a classic example of British architecture. Built upon the banks of the Hooghly river, it was dedicated to Queen Victoria, the then Empress of India.

Brihadisvara Temple

Also known as Peruvudaiyār Kōvil, this is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva in Thanjavur. This was one of the tallest structures in the world at the time of its construction.

Kanchenjunga
